Introducing ORH: Diagnostic Power, Unchained

ORH is a revolutionary medical device built on robotics, AI, and mechatronics to solve these problems, fundamentally redefining the X-ray radiological industry.

Distance Imaging

The ORH Difference: It is possible to get a clear, diagnostic-quality X-ray image from a distance.

Impact & Benefit: This enables non-intrusive diagnosis for home-hospitalized people and those with reduced mobility, dramatically improving patient comfort and care quality.

Complete Wireless Mobility

The ORH Difference: The device does not require plugging into any source of electrical power and is effortless to transport.

Impact & Benefit: This facilitates a seamless transition to mobile healthcare, simplifies in-office procedures, and allows imaging anywhere it's needed.

Enhanced Safety

The ORH Difference: It ensures operator safety without the need for cumbersome, sealed protection.

Impact & Benefit: This streamlines operations, improves operator comfort, reduces setup time, and ensures a faster overall operation processing.
